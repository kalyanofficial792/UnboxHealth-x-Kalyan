About UNBOX HEALTH
No opinions.
Only facts.
India's first independent ratings platform for foods & supplements

Unbox Health tests packaged foods and health supplements through NABL-accredited labs — sourcing every product directly from open markets, never from brands. Each product is graded on three objective criteria: Label Accuracy, Non-Toxicity, and Nutritional Profile, producing a final rating of D to A+. All lab reports are public.

The Unmarked USP

No expert panels. No influencer endorsements. No brand involvement. Unbox runs on zero opinion — ratings emerge purely from lab data mapped against FSSAI & ICMR benchmarks. This radical factfulness is the brand's most powerful differentiator — and its most under-marketed one.
